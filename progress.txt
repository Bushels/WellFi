# WellFi Marketing Page - Progress Log

This file is a current-state handoff, not a historical archive. Remove stale hero direction when the approved sequence changes.

## Current Approved Hero State

### Startup Sequence
- ultra-faint ghost baseline
- fixed-height left-to-right sine-wave sweep
- WellFi logo appears at 75% sweep
- `Stop Pumping Blind` lands at full sweep
- subheadline, proof chips, and CTAs reveal only after the headline
- mouse/touch interaction turns on only after the sweep completes
- hero logo arcs can pulse on direct hover/touch/focus without adding a looping distraction

### Regression Triggers To Avoid
- bottom-up band motion during intro
- changing wave height, spread, or amplitude during intro
- spark bursts, lens spikes, or other cinematic startup effects
- revealing copy before the sweep completes
- hardcoded GSAP times that drift away from the shared wave duration
- separate mouse and touch codepaths that drift apart instead of one pointer-event path
- large hero dead space caused by full-screen min-height choices that do not serve the layout

### Maintenance Rule
- When hero startup behavior changes, update both `docs/hero-startup-animation.md` and `CLAUDE.md` in the same pass.
- When Playwright is used for verification, keep artifacts out of git and only retain them intentionally.
