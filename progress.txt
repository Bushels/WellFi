## Session: Initial Planning (Current Date)

### Decisions Made
- Tagline selected: "Wireless Below. Insight Above."
- Stack: Next.js 14 (App Router) + Tailwind + GSAP ScrollTrigger
- Deployment: Vercel (will link from MPS Wix site)
- Dark mode first design approach

### Key Product Insights
- Fiberglass collar is THE key differentiator - creates electrical isolation
- Two 10ft pups minimum with wire-wheeled bare steel contact
- Signal path: through formation, not short-circuit through continuous steel
- DeltaPressure = continuous monitoring + scheduled transmission + exception alerts

### Target Audience Notes
- Cold production engineers in Canadian oilsands
- Clearwater, Blue Sky, Grand Rapids formations
- Entry point: pump changeouts (minimal additional rig time)
- They know their pain points - we prompt, they conclude

### Content Guidelines
- No specific cost comparisons (competitor pricing uncertain)
- Can say "130+ installs" but not specific customer names
- Let engineers draw their own conclusions about benefits
- Use industry terms correctly: MODBUS, RTU, SCADA, PCP

### Section Messaging Finalized
1. Hero: "Wireless Below. Insight Above."
2. Benefits: "Reliable. Simple. Powerful." (icon grid)
3. Technology: "Electromagnetic Telemetry — The New Standard"
4. Product: "Modular Design. Engineered Integrity."
5. Installation: "Quicker Install. No Strings Attached."
6. Integration: "Plug and Play"
7. Intelligence: "Deploy and Forget. We'll Alert You."
8. Results: "Less Downtime. Fewer Workovers. Optimized Production."
9. Specs: Engineer-focused detail section
10. CTA: "Become Wireless" + "Contact Us Now"
